The Fruity Fun Your Kids Will Love!

Say goodbye to artificial colors and hello to natural goodness! Burple is the delicious fruit drink crafted to bring smiles to little faces, made with real fruit and no compromises.

The Burple kids' drink was a popular children’s beverage in the 1980s and 1990s, known for its unique expandable accordion-style plastic bottle. It came in a variety of fruity flavours that made it fun and appealing to kids.

BURPLE is a splash of fruity fun created just for kids! Bursting with bold flavors, bright colors, and a bubbly personality, BURPLE is more than a drink—it's an adventure in every bottle. Whether it's a school lunchbox surprise or a summer playdate treat, BURPLE brings smiles, energy, and a big burst of fruit-powered joy.
